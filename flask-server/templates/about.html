ABSTRACT

The goal of this project is to employ natural language processing to assess consumer complaints about a product in order to give the manufacturer useful information about how to enhance the product. Customers can express their thoughts and experiences about a product thanks to the rising popularity of internet forums, which makes it simpler for businesses to get consumer feedback. Yet, it can be difficult to analyse huge amounts of consumer complaint data. By applying natural language processing techniques to automate the analysis of consumer complaint data, this research seeks to address this problem. Consumer complaint data will be gathered for the project from a variety of sources, including social networking sites, online review sites, and customer service channels. The data is subsequently processed and cleaned to remove extraneous data and noise. Techniques for natural language processing are used to determine the tone of each complaint. The analysis's results are used to inform the maker of the product's flaws and offer suggestions for improvement. This project offers two advantages. In the beginning, it offers producers insightful information on the consumer experience, assisting them in determining where their product needs to be improved. Second, it gives users a tool for automating the analysis of customer complaint data, which saves time and effort compared to manually processing and analysing huge amounts of data. This project has the potential to greatly improve consumer satisfaction and experience, which will increase sales and brand loyalty for the company.
